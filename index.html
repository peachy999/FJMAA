Meet The Team



Our Mission

To improve the health status and awareness of Fujianese Americans and to promote excellence in health care for all through the mobilization of health care professionals.

Purpose & Objectives

To eliminate health disparities of Fujianese Americans and other underserved populations.

To advance medical knowledge through education, scientific research, scholarship, and philanthropy with emphasis on aspects unique to Fujianese Americans.

To promote and to advocate for medical professionals of Fujianese descent and others devoted to like purposes.
